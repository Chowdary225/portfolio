üì± How to Create Certificate Images

Method 1: Screenshot (Easiest)

  1. Open your certificate PDF
  2. Take a screenshot of the entire certificate
  3. Save as JPG with the exact filename above
  4. Copy to /public/certificates/images/

Method 2: PDF Export

  1. Open PDF in Preview (Mac) or Adobe Reader
  2. Go to File ‚Üí Export ‚Üí JPEG
  3. Choose high quality settings
  4. Rename to exact filename above
  5. Copy to /public/certificates/images/

Method 3: Online Converter

  1. Use tools like PDF24, ILovePDF, or SmallPDF
  2. Upload your certificate PDF
  3. Convert to JPG/PNG
  4. Download and rename correctly
  5. Copy to /public/certificates/images/
